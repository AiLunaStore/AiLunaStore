1
Resuscitation - Immediate life threat
2
Emergent - High risk, confused, severe pain
3
Urgent - Multiple resources needed
4
Less Urgent - One resource needed
5
Non-Urgent - No resources needed

HR >90 bpm
RR >20/min
WBC >12K or <4K
Glucose >140
Altered mental status

🧠 GCS Quick Reference
Eye Opening (1-4)
4=Spont, 3=Voice, 2=Pain, 1=None
Verbal (1-5)
5=Oriented, 4=Confused, 3=Words, 2=Sounds, 1=None
Motor (1-6)
6=Commands, 5=Localize, 4=Withdraw, 3=Flexion, 2=Extension, 1=None
