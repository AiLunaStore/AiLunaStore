# Quality Assurance Metrics

## Purpose
Measure and track quality across all work products and processes.

## Quality Dimensions

### 1. Correctness
Work is accurate and error-free.

### 2. Completeness
Work addresses all requirements.

### 3. Clarity
Work is understandable and well-organized.

### 4. Consistency
Work follows established patterns and standards.

### 5. Maintainability
Work can be easily updated and extended.
