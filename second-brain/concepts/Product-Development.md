# Product Development

Product Development encompasses the full lifecycle from ideation and research through launch and iteration — including [[GitHub-Management]] for code delivery, [[Knowledge-Management]] for product specs and decision logs, and [[LeadQualify-AI]] for customer-driven feature prioritization. Modern product dev also leverages AI tools like [[Fakery-AI]] and [[Canva-Templates-Research]] for rapid prototyping and marketing asset production. Effective product development requires tight integration between [[Delegation-Pattern]] and [[Memory-System]] to maintain context across teams and sessions.
