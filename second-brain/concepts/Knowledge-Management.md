# Knowledge Management

Knowledge Management is the practice of capturing, organizing, and surfacing information so it remains accessible and useful over time. In a personal or team second brain, this means maintaining structured notes, linking related concepts, and regularly reviewing and pruning content. Tools like Obsidian with its graph view make implicit connections explicit through [[wiki-links]], enabling emergent discovery. It connects to [[Memory-System]] for personal continuity, [[Product-Development]] for institutional memory, and [[OpenClaw-Setup]] for maintaining skill documentation and workspace knowledge.
