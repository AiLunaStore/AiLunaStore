# LeadQualify AI

LeadQualify AI refers to systems and workflows for scoring, qualifying, and routing sales leads using AI-driven analysis of prospect data and behavior signals. The goal is to prioritize high-intent leads for human sales follow-up while automating initial qualification stages. Typical inputs include firmographic data, engagement metrics, and conversational transcripts. It connects to [[Fakery-AI]] for synthetic lead testing, [[Product-Development]] for building qualification features, and [[Knowledge-Management]] for maintaining playbooks and qualification criteria.
