# Fakery AI

Fakery AI is an AI tool or system used for generating realistic-looking but fictional content — commonly applied in prototyping, mockups, and creative testing scenarios. It enables rapid generation of fake data, images, text, and personas for design reviews, demos, and placeholder content. The name suggests a focus on synthetic media with controlled parameters for realism vs. obvious artificiality. It connects to [[LeadQualify-AI]] for generating synthetic lead personas during qualification testing, [[Canva-Templates-Research]] for mockup asset creation, and [[Product-Development]] for prototyping workflows.
