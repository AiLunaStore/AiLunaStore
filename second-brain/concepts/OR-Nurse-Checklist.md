# OR Nurse Checklist

The OR Nurse Checklist is a systematic pre-procedure verification protocol used in operating rooms to ensure patient safety, surgical site confirmation, and equipment readiness. It typically follows WHO Surgical Safety Checklist guidelines with additions for specialty surgical procedures and facility-specific requirements. The checklist serves as a final verification before incision, reducing preventable errors and improving communication among the surgical team. Related to [[Delegation-Pattern]] in how tasks are systematically assigned and confirmed, and [[Knowledge-Management]] for capturing institutional best practices.
