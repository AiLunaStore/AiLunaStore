# Canva Templates Research

Canva Templates Research involves investigating existing Canva template libraries, analyzing design patterns, and identifying gaps for custom template creation. This supports brand-consistent content production at scale by pre-building assets for social media, presentations, and marketing materials. Research includes studying typography, color systems, layout grids, and component modularity across template categories. It connects to [[Fakery-AI]] and [[LeadQualify-AI]] for marketing and brand asset needs, and [[Product-Development]] for building template-based product workflows.
