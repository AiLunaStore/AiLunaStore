# MedSurg Checklist Brain Sheet Design Deliverable

## Task Summary
Create a detailed design plan for a MedSurg checklist brain sheet based on research findings of common options nurses repeatedly write. Design includes checklist/circle options, logical categories, balance for 80% of cases plus space for unusual cases, and a human outline for marking medical equipment locations.

## Deliverables Produced

### 1. Design Plan (`Medsurg_Checklist_Brain_Sheet_Design_Plan.md`)
- Comprehensive overview of design principles, layout, color coding, typography, and visual cues.
- Specifies portrait 8.5×11" single‑page format with two‑column layout (70% checklists, 30% human outline).
- Details ADHD‑friendly features and MedSurg‑specific checklist content.

### 2. Specifications (`Medsurg_Checklist_Brain_Sheet_Specifications.md`)
- Detailed section‑by‑section breakdown of checklist options, layout, color schemes.
- Includes technical specifications for each category (Mental Status, IV Fluids, Mobility, Pain Scale, Common Meds, Assessments, Vitals/I&O, Human Outline, Notes).
- Customizability guidelines and validation criteria.

### 3. Layout Diagram (`Medsurg_Checklist_Brain_Sheet_Layout_Diagram.txt`)
- ASCII diagram visualizing the overall layout and section flow.
- Quick reference for structure, checklist options, and human outline integration.

### 4. HTML Mockup (`medsurg_checklist_brain_sheet_mockup.html`)
- Interactive HTML/CSS mockup with functional checkboxes, pain scale selection, and human outline diagram.
- Demonstrates the two‑column layout, color‑coded sections, and print‑ready styling.
- Can be viewed in any browser and printed for paper‑based testing.

## Key Design Features

### 1. **Checklist‑Based Documentation**
- Replaces repetitive handwriting with pre‑defined checkboxes and circles.
- Covers 80% of typical MedSurg cases with common options.
- Includes free‑text lines for unusual or patient‑specific details.

### 2. **Logical Categorization**
- **Mental Status**: A&O levels, consciousness states.
- **IV Fluids**: Fluid type, rate, site, status.
- **Mobility**: Ambulation, assistance, turning schedule, fall precautions.
- **Pain Scale**: Visual 0–10 scale with descriptors and intervention tracking.
- **Common Meds**: Frequently administered medications grouped by class.
- **Assessments**: Neuro, wound care, drains, respiratory, GI/GU.
- **Vitals/I&O**: Compact grid for four timepoints plus cumulative totals.

### 3. **Human Outline Integration**
- Simple front‑view silhouette for visual mapping of:
  - IV sites (●)
  - Foley catheter (▲)
  - Wounds/dressings (✕)
  - Drains (○)
  - Pressure injuries (■)
- Left/Right notation and landmark dots for precise location marking.

### 4. **ADHD‑Friendly Visual Design**
- Color‑coded sections:
  - Green: Mental Status
  - Blue: IV Fluids
  - Yellow: Mobility
  - Red: Pain Scale
  - Purple: Common Meds
  - Teal: Assessments
  - Gray: Vitals/I&O & Notes
- Checklist mentality with completion boxes.
- Minimalist design, high contrast, consistent layout.

### 5. **MedSurg‑Specific Content**
- Derived from research on common options nurses repeatedly write.
- Balanced to reduce documentation time while maintaining clinical accuracy.
- Professional appearance suitable for hospital environment.
