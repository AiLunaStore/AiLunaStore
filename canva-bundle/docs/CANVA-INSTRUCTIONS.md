# Canva Instructions — How to Recreate Each Template

## Step 1: Setting Up Your Canva Account

1. Go to [canva.com](https://www.canva.com) and sign up (free)
2. For full features, upgrade to Canva Pro ($12.99/month) — **recommended**
3. All templates work on free tier unless noted

## Step 2: Creating a Design with Custom Dimensions

### Instagram Post (1080×1080)
1. Click **"Create a design"**
2. Select **"Custom dimensions"**
3. Enter: Width `1080`, Height `1080`
4. Click **"Create new design"**

### Instagram Story (1080×1920)
1. Click **"Create a design"**
2. Select **"Custom dimensions"**
3. Enter: Width `1080`, Height `1920`
4. Click **"Create new design"**

### LinkedIn Banner (1584×396)
1. Click **"Create a design"**
2. Select **"Custom dimensions"**
3. Enter: Width `1584`, Height `396`
4. Click **"Create new design"**

### YouTube Thumbnail (1280×720)
1. Click **"Create a design"**
2. Select **"Custom dimensions"**
3. Enter: Width `1280`, Height `720`
4. Click **"Create new design"**

### Presentation (1920×1080)
1. Click **"Create a design"**
2. Search for **"Presentation (16:9)"**
3. Or enter: Width `1920`, Height `1080`

## Step 3: Finding & Applying Fonts

### Font Pairings Used in This Bundle

**Montserrat + Open Sans:**
1. In Canva editor, click **"Text"** → **"Add a heading"**
2. Click the font name in the text toolbar
3. Search for **"Montserrat"**
4. Select **Montserrat Bold** for headings, **Montserrat SemiBold** for subheads

**For body text:**
1. Add **"Add a subtitle"** or **"Add a body text"**
2. Change font to **"Open Sans"** or **"Lato"**

**For accent/quote text:**
1. Add **"Add a quote"** or heading
2. Change font to **"Playfair Display"** or **"Cormorant"** (italic)

## Step 7: Exporting Your Designs

### For PDF (Etsy customers)
1. Click **"Share"** button
2. Click **"Download"**
3. Select **PDF Print** for high quality
4. Choose **"Fit"** or **"Fill"** bleed options

### For Canva Links (Gumroad customers)
1. Click **"Share"** button
2. Set link permissions to **"Canva free editor"** or **"Anyone with link"**
3. Copy the share link
4. Provide this link to customers

### For PNG/JPG
1. Click **"Download"**
2. Select **PNG** or **JPG**
3. Choose quality (Best recommended)
4. Download

---

## Step 8: Batch Creating

To speed up customization:

1. Create a **"Master Template"** in Canva
2. Click **"Use this design"** to duplicate
3. Edit the duplicate (don't affect original)
4. Repeat for each client/variation

## Troubleshooting

**Q: Fonts look different in Canva**
A: Canva substitutes fonts when your chosen font isn't available. Use "Manage fonts" to download Montserrat and other Google Fonts.

**Q: Colors aren't exact**
A: Always use hex codes, not color picker. In Canva, click the color → "Enter hex code" → paste exact value.

**Q: Design looks blurry**
A: Ensure you're using 1080px+ dimensions and downloading at highest quality (PDF Print, not PDF Standard).

## Advanced Tips

1. **Use Canva's Magic Write** (Pro) to generate text variations
2. **Duplicate element groups** for consistent layouts
3. **Lock elements** you don't want accidentally moved
4. **Use grids** (View → Show Guides) for alignment
5. **Create your own templates** by saving any design as a template
