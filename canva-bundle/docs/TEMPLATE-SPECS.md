## Spacing & Layout Reference

### Instagram Post (1080×1080) — Safe Zones
- Outer margin: 60-80px
- Content inset: 60px from all edges
- Text blocks: max-width 85% of canvas
- Center-aligned elements: optical centering (slightly above mathematical center)

### Instagram Story (1080×1920) — Safe Zones
- Top: 60px (above Instagram UI)
- Bottom: 200px (above DM/share bar)
- Side margins: 40-50px
- CTA button: bottom 300px zone

### LinkedIn Banner (1584×396) — Layouts
- Left-aligned text: 80px from left
- Right-aligned elements: 80px from right
- Vertical center: roughly 198px from top
- Profile photo zone: rightmost 300px

### YouTube Thumbnail (1280×720) — Visibility Rules
- Keep text in LEFT 70% of frame (right side often has timestamp/UI)
- Face photos: place in lower-right quadrant
- Big numbers: bold, high contrast, optical center
- All text: stroke/outline for small-screen visibility
