# Quick Start Guide — 15 Minutes to Your First Post

## The 15-Minute Plan

### Minute 1-3: Set Up
1. Purchase bundle → Download ZIP
2. Open the PDF guide
3. Click your Canva template link
4. Create free Canva account (if needed)

### Minute 4-8: Pick & Open
1. Browse the template categories
2. Find an Instagram post you like
3. Click "Use this design"
4. The template opens in Canva editor

### Minute 9-12: Customize
1. Click any text to edit → Type your own
2. Click colors to change → Use your brand colors
3. Upload your logo → Drag onto the template
4. Change images → Upload your own photos

### Minute 13-15: Export & Post
1. Click "Share" → "Download"
2. Select PNG or JPG (Best quality)
3. Save to your phone or computer
4. Post to Instagram/LinkedIn/YouTube!

## Pro Tips

1. **Batch create** — Do all your content design on Sunday afternoon
2. **Use Canva's Brand Kit** — Set your colors/fonts once, apply everywhere
3. **Preview on phone** — What looks good on desktop might look different on mobile
4. **Keep text minimal** — Social posts with less text perform better
5. **Consistency beats perfection** — Post something over posting nothing

## Troubleshooting

**Q: Template looks different in Canva**
A: Canva updates its interface regularly. The templates are designed correctly — just look for the same layout elements.

**Q: Can't find a specific template**
A: Use the template naming guide in README.md to locate designs.

**Q: Colors aren't matching exactly**
A: Use hex codes from the template spec sheet. Don't use the color picker — type the hex code manually.

**Q: Font isn't loading**
A: Canva needs internet connection to load fonts. Also try clicking the font name and searching again.

**Q: How do I share templates with clients?**
A: Use Canva's "Share" → "Invite people" to give clients edit access to specific designs.
