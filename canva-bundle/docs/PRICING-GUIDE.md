## Launch Strategy (First 7 Days)

### Day 1-2: Soft Launch
- Post to your own social media
- Email your existing list
- Offer to 10-20 beta buyers at 50% off
- Collect testimonials

### Day 3-5: Public Launch
- Post to all social platforms
- Launch paid ads (if budget allows)
- Reach out to affiliates/partners

### Day 6-7: Urgency
- Remove launch discount
- Announce price increase
- Send "last chance" email

## Competitor Pricing Research

| Competitor | Price | What's Included |
|------------|-------|-----------------|
| Generic Canva bundle | $19-29 | 20-30 templates, 1 palette |
| Premium coach bundle | $47-67 | 30-50 templates, multiple palettes |
| Agency/pro template kit | $97-197 | 100+ templates, brand kit, tutorials |
| Custom brand design | $2,000+ | Full brand identity |

**Your positioning:** Premium mid-market. Better quality than $19 bundles, better value than $197 agency kits. Position as "professional brand designer quality at a DIY price."

---

## Psychological Pricing

- **Use .97 instead of round numbers:** $27.97 vs $28
- **Anchoring:** Show original price crossed out ($97 → $27)
- **Urgency:** "Launch price — goes up Friday"
- **Bundle pricing:** "Get both Etsy + Gumroad versions for $10 more"

## Key Positioning Messages

### For Instagram/Facebook Ads:
"Your coaching expertise is premium. Your social media should be too."
"41 templates. 5 platforms. One professional brand."
"Looks like you hired a designer. Actually took 15 minutes in Canva."

### For SEO/Descriptions:
"Premium social media templates for coaches and consultants"
"Canva templates that actually look professional"
"Social media kit for coaches who want to look credible"

### For Testimonials to Ask:
"What was your experience like when you first used the templates?"
"Would you recommend this to a fellow coach? Why or why not?"
"How did your social media look before vs. after using these templates?"
