## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I need Canva Pro?**
No! All templates work perfectly with the free Canva account. Canva Pro unlocks additional features but isn't required.

**Can I use these for client work?**
These templates are licensed for your personal/business use. You can create content for your own brand or your clients' brands, but you cannot resell or redistribute the template files.

**What format do I receive?**
You receive **editable Canva links** — you can access and edit all templates directly in Canva through your browser. No software to install.

**Can I use my own fonts?**
Yes! All templates use Canva's built-in free fonts (Montserrat, Open Sans, Playfair Display). You can also upload your own brand fonts in Canva Pro.

**How do I get access?**
After purchase on Gumroad, you'll receive an email with your unique Canva template link. Create a free Canva account (or log in) and the templates are ready to edit.
