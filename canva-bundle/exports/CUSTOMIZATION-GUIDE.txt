## How to Edit

### In Canva (Recommended)
1. Open Canva and create a new design with matching dimensions
2. Recreate the design using the PNG/PDF as a reference
3. Update text with your own content
4. Change colors to match your brand if desired

### In Photoshop/Illustrator
1. Open any PNG/PDF file
2. Edit text and elements as needed
3. Export at original dimensions

---

## Tips for Success

- **Consistency matters** — Use 1-2 color palettes consistently across your feed
- **Brand voice** — Adapt the placeholder text to match your unique voice
- **Testimonials** — Replace with your actual client testimonials
- **Call to action** — Always include a clear next step for your audience
