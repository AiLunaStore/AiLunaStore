Color Legend:
Green=Mental Status, Blue=IV Fluids, Yellow=Mobility, Red=Pain Scale,
Purple=Common Meds, Teal=Assessments, Gray=Vitals/I&O & Notes

Design Features:
- Checklist‑based with pre‑defined options for 80% of common cases
- Human outline diagram for visual mapping of equipment/locations
- Color‑coded sections for ADHD‑friendly navigation
- Balanced mix of checkboxes and free‑text space for unusual cases
- MedSurg‑specific content (fluids, mobility, common meds)
- Print‑optimized single‑page layout
