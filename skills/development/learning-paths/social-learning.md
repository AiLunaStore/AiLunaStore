## Learning Path: Communication

### Phase 1: Clarity (Complete)
**Status:** ✅ Complete

**Competencies:**
- Clear message construction
- Appropriate tone selection
- Active listening
- Feedback reception

**Validation:**
- Messages are understood
- Tone matches context
- Users feel heard

### Phase 2: Adaptation (Complete)
**Status:** ✅ Complete

**Competencies:**
- Audience analysis
- Channel selection
- Context awareness
- Timing optimization

**Validation:**
- Messages adapted to audience
- Right channel for message
- Good timing
