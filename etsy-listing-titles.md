# Etsy Listing Title Options — Canva Template Bundles for Coaches & Businesses

## Key Research Findings

### Etsy Title Guidelines (2025–2026)
- **Target: under 15 words** (Etsy updated guidance Jan 2026)
- Front-load keywords — most important info first
- Skip filler words: "perfect," "beautiful," "amazing" → move to description
- No repetition of keywords
- No price/shipping info — Etsy adds this automatically
- State what the item IS clearly upfront (e.g., "Instagram templates," "social media bundle")
- Add key details early: format, quantity, niche

### Top-Performing Listing Title Patterns (from Etsy best sellers)
1. **Quantity + Niche + Platform + Format** → `60 Wellness Coach Instagram Canva Templates`
2. **Niche + Platform + Format Bundle** → `Canva Bundle Social Media Templates for Branding Coach`
3. **MEGA/ULTIMATE BUNDLE + Year + Niche** → `2025 MEGA BUNDLE Coaching Business Template`
4. **Use Case + Platform + (Optional: Edition/Version)** → `Instagram Coach Canva Templates: Social Media Toolkit`

## Notes on Title Strategy

| Strategy | Do | Don't |
|---|---|---|
| **Length** | Under 15 words | Strings of 20+ keywords |
| **Word order** | What it is → niche → format → platform | Brand name first, vague opener |
| **Filler** | Use "editable," "bundle," "template" | Use "perfect," "stunning," "amazing" |
| **Separators** | Pipe ` \| ` or comma to break sections | Dash `–` or long unbroken strings |
| **Repetition** | Vary synonyms (posts vs. templates vs. content) | Repeat "Canva" 3+ times |
| **Price/promo** | Never in title | No "best price," "sale," "discount" |

## Recommended A/B Testing Approach
Test 2–3 titles simultaneously, track views + favorites over 30 days. Prioritize titles that front-load **primary keyword + niche + format** within the first 8 words.
