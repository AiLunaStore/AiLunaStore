Legend: Red=Hemodynamics, Blue=Respiratory, Green=Neuro, Yellow=I&O, Purple=Labs, Gray=Notes

Design Features:
- 24-hour tiered grid with shaded alternating hours
- Color-coded sections for ADHD-friendly navigation
- Integrated drip and vent flow sheets
- Checklist mentality for task completion
- Trend arrows and icon indicators for abnormalities
- MedSurg/ER aesthetic consistency (clean, professional)
